Cog is a tool to simplify getting models up and running on your local machine, using the power of Docker. They're also packagable and deployable to Replicate, where you can serve them easily, at scale, to as many people as you need.

Let's have a look at how to:

- Set up cog locally
- Initialise a project
- Get a model running
- Tweak the JSON we get back from the model depending on how successful it feels
- Deploy it to Replicate
- Get a response from a web app
